E-commerce Scaling

Hyper-Scaling an E-commerce Brand to E£2.28M

Objective: Aggressive revenue scaling while maintaining backend profitability via Meta Ads to Shopify.

The Strategy: Leveraging my analytical background, I implemented a strict testing framework to identify the highest-converting audiences before scaling. We consolidated the account to allow the Meta Pixel to season rapidly. Once Shopify CVR stabilized, we executed aggressive vertical scaling on winning CBO campaigns.

The Outcome: We drove 80K+ highly qualified sessions, avoiding the usual drop in efficiency during high-spend phases.
